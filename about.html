Our Team

Meet the dedicated professionals behind SpineDocs

A Patient-Oriented Practice

SpineDocs is committed to providing objective, transparent, evidence-based spinal medicine. Our team leverages the latest and most advanced surgical products and techniques while prioritizing minimally invasive procedures whenever feasible.

We believe in empowering patients through education. We provide comprehensive information about conditions and all available treatment options—both surgical and non-surgical—enabling you to make informed decisions about your care.

Lead Surgeon

Stanley Lee, MD

Board Certified Orthopedic Spine Surgeon

Dr. Stanley Lee is committed to providing the highest level of care for patients with spinal disorders. He specializes in treating spinal conditions through evidence-based medicine with an emphasis on transparency, objectivity, and patient education.

Dr. Lee always explores nonsurgical treatments before recommending any type of surgery, ensuring patients understand all their options and can make informed decisions about their care.
