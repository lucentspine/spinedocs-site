What We Treat

Conditions & Services

Comprehensive spine care from diagnosis through recovery

Minimally Invasive Surgery

Advanced surgical techniques using smaller incisions, resulting in less blood loss, reduced pain, smaller scars, and faster recovery times.

Spinal Stenosis

Treatment for narrowing of the spinal canal that causes nerve compression, pain, numbness, or weakness in the back and legs.

Disc Herniation

Relief for bulging or ruptured discs that press on spinal nerves, causing pain that radiates into arms or legs.

Degenerative Disc Disease

Care for age-related wear and breakdown of spinal discs that can cause chronic back pain and reduced mobility.

Disc Replacement

Surgical replacement of damaged discs with artificial implants, preserving natural spinal movement unlike fusion surgery.

Radiculopathy

Treatment for pinched nerves in the spine causing pain, tingling, or weakness that radiates along the nerve path.

Compression Fractures

Care for vertebral fractures often caused by osteoporosis, trauma, or other conditions affecting bone strength.

Neck & Back Pain

Comprehensive evaluation and treatment for acute and chronic pain affecting your spine and quality of life.
